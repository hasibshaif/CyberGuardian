About

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting internet-connected systems, including hardware, software, and data, from cyber threats. It encompasses various measures and technologies aimed at safeguarding individuals,organizations, and nations against malicious activities such as unauthorized access, data breaches, and cyberattacks. In today's interconnected world, cybersecurity awareness and implementation are essential for ensuring the safety and privacy of online users and their digital assets. With cybercrimes on the rise, the need for effective cybersecurity is more crucial now than ever. CyberGuardian is a platform that provides resources and information to help individuals and organizations take proactive measures to protect their digital assets.

According to Statista Market Insights, the cost from cybercrime worldwide is expected to rise to 13.82 trillion dollars in 2028.

Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is a form of harassment or intimidation that takes place online, typically through social media, messaging platforms, or other digital channels. It can manifest in various ways, including spreading rumors, sharing embarrassing photos or videos, or sending threatening messages. This insidious behavior not only undermines the victim's confidence and self-esteem but also leads to profound emotional distress and, in extreme cases, can result in depression or even suicide. If you or someone you know has been a victim, text HOME to 741741
